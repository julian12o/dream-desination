Tokyo, Japan

東京、日本 Tokyo, city and capital of Tokyo of Japan. It is located at the head of Tokyo Bay on the Pacific coast of central Honshu.

Reasons why you should go to Japan.

Japan is very clean, Japanese people are polite and friendly, The incredible natural landscapes, The spectacular cherry blossom season, The superb Japanese cuisine, The fantastic architecture, The advanced technology, The great fashion, Japan is very safe.
